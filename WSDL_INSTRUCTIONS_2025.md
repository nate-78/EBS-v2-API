# WSDL Instructions 2025
These are the instructions the IRS included when they sent the latest WSDL files.

The “AIR FS26-TY25 Production WSDL Package Jan 2026_v1.0” package is to be used for Production transmissions when AIR Production opens for Filing Season 2025 in January 2025.

The “AIR FS26-TY25 AATS WSDL Package Nov 2025_v1.0” package is to be used for AATS transmissions when AATS opens for Filing Season 2026 in November 2025.

Please make note of the following:
Tax Year 2024 is considered a prior year return in AATS once AATS opens for FS26 in November 2025.
Tax Year 2024 is considered a prior year return in Production when Production opens for FS26 in January 2026.

All transmissions to AATS and Production must use the Tax Year 2025 Manifest, including prior year returns 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023 and 2024 beginning in November 2025 for AATS, and January 2026 for Production.
